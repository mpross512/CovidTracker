About

This website was developed by Group 15 for our Spring 2021 3354 Software Engineering project.

The purpose of this website is to provide a heatmap of Covid-19 related data so users can easily see which areas are affected the most.

While the full functionality is not present, we did develop it according to our specified requirements.
